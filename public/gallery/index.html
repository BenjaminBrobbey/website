My Trip to Scotland

2021-11-01 Travelling Holidays Lednerb
In June 2017 I’ve visited Scotland with my best friend for two weeks. We drove a lot through the beautiful countryside and saw a lot of different animals: cattle, deer, birds, dolphins, and sheep. Sheep everywhere! At one beautiful camping site, we saw cattle in fog in the very early morning and took a picture of it. The image now has the title “Rindviecher im Nebel”, a quite funny German phrase that means cattle in the fog. Continue reading
